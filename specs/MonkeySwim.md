This is going to be an Android-native game that is almost identical in gameplay to Pac-Man.

Key differences:
- There will be multiple levels
    - The first level will look essentially identical to the original Pac-Man maze. We will add additional levels as we fine-tune gameplay.
    - In addition to the two gateways on the left and right that send the monkey to the other side of the screen, there will be a gateway at the bottom that is locked off. As soon as the last pellet is eaten by the monkey, the bottom gateway will unlock, and once the monkey enters they will be transported to the next level.
- The main character is a monkey
- Rather than a plain arcade maze, each level is a water level, with the monkey swimming around canals and islands to collect 
- The main enemies are piranhas, rather than ghosts.

Gameplay specs:

- Render all sprites of the monkey and piranhas swimming up, down, left, and right, with transitional elements. 
- The player is granted three lives. Once the lives are used up, a splash screen is presented offering the player the opportunity to either start the game over, or watch an ad (via Google AdMob) and gain three additional lives.

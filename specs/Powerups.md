Periodically a banana will appear somewhere on the map.

If the player collects the banana, one of the following powerups will occur:

- All ghosts are hit by a lightning bolt and are instantly transported back to the pen, and then they will be released on the same schedule as when the level first starts.
- A black hole will appear somewhere on the board. While it is active it slowly drags the piranhas toward it from around the maze. Any character that comes in contact with it will be killed.
- A shark will appear and chase the piranha. If it comes in contact, the piranha is killed.
- All piranha speeds will be reduced by 50% for ten seconds (or until the player dies or a next level starts), but the player's speed is unaffected. For this effect, a turtle will appear on the screen briefly, similar to the white powerup in Brick Basher (also installed on this computer).
- Player gets an extra life. This is accompanied by flashing a heart on the screen, similar to what happens when you get the pink powerup in Brick Basher.
- Player is granted a **bait charge** rather than an instant effect. While charges > 0 a 🐟 button appears in the HUD; tapping it drops a bait fish on the monkey's current tile. Every piranha in CHASE mode (anywhere in the maze) detours to the bait; the bait disappears when a piranha walks onto it, or after 6 seconds. The first time the player is granted a bait charge, the game pauses and shows a one-time tutorial popup explaining what bait is and how to use it; on every later grant a hooked-worm icon briefly pops centre-screen (like the turtle/heart powerup icons). Only one bait is active at a time; charges accumulate (multiple BAIT rolls stack). Charges persist across level transitions, life loss, and save/restore.

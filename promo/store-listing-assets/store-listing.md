## Full description

```
Dive into the rapids! Monkey Rapids is a fast, friendly arcade maze-chase
with a splash of jungle river adventure. Help a brave little monkey swim
through winding canals, gobble up every piece of tasty fruit, and stay one
fin ahead of the piranhas snapping at his tail.

It's the classic chase-the-maze arcade feeling you love — reimagined
underwater. Easy to pick up, tricky to master, and full of surprises the
deeper downstream you go.

HOW IT PLAYS
• Swipe to swim — simple one-thumb controls anyone can play.
• Clear every fruit in the maze to open the glowing portal to the next level.
• Four colorful piranhas chase you, each with its own personality.
• Bite a golden power-fruit to turn the tables: the piranhas panic and flee,
  and you can chomp them down for big bonus points.

GRAB A BANANA, GET A POWER-UP
A banana drifts into the maze every so often. Snag it for a wild, random
power-up:
• Lightning — zaps every piranha back to its pen.
• Black hole — drags the piranhas in and swallows them whole.
• Shark — a hungry predator that hunts the piranhas for you.
• Turtle — slows the whole swarm to a crawl.
• Extra life — one more shot at the river.
• Bait — a fish you can drop on demand to lure the piranhas away when you're
  cornered.

A RIVER FULL OF HAZARDS
The deeper you swim, the stranger the river gets:
• Currents that sweep you and the piranhas along their lanes.
• A slow, relentless crocodile patrolling the maze — power-fruit doesn't
  scare it, so time your crossing!
• Rising and falling tides that open and seal secret passages.
• Slippery lily pads you can't stop or turn on once you slide aboard.

PLENTY TO CONQUER
• 30 hand-crafted levels, each introducing new layouts and twists.
• Three difficulty settings — Easy, Medium, and Hard.
• Cleared them all? Challenge Mode loops you back faster and fiercer, with an
  ever-growing swarm of piranhas.

MADE WITH CARE
• Crisp, hand-styled visuals drawn fresh for every screen size.
• Punchy original sound effects.
• Plays fully offline — no account, no sign-up.
• Out of lives? Watch a quick optional video to jump right back in.

Take a deep breath, line up your swipes, and clear the rapids. The river is
waiting — and so are the piranhas.

```
